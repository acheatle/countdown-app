About

Countdowns

Create countdowns with a target date and time. The display adapts based on urgency:

  • >7 days — Shows days remaining with date
  • ≤7 days — Adds day name for context
  • ≤36 hours — Switches to hours
  • ≤60 minutes — Shows minutes

Three lists organize your countdowns: Active (in progress), Archive (completed), and Canceled.

When a countdown ends, you'll be asked if you met your timeline. Say yes to archive it, or extend/cancel if you need more time.

Projects

Track ongoing work and recurring meetings without deadlines. Each project stores links and notes in one place.

Use the tab switcher at the top to switch between Countdowns and Projects.

Privacy & Data

  • Your data is stored locally in your browser
  • Nobody else can see your data, not even the app creator
  • Each device has separate data unless you manually sync

Syncing Across Devices

  1. Use Export to download your data as a JSON file
  2. Save the file somewhere accessible (iCloud, email, etc.)
  3. Use Import on other devices to load that data

Both countdowns and projects will transfer exactly, including all links and notes.
